Albert Einstein

About me

Iam Albert Einstein.I was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany.Iam a theoretical physicist from Ulm, Germany.I excelled at physics and mathematics from an early age, and soon acquired the mathematical expertise normally only found in a child several years his senior.I began teaching myself algebra, calculus and Euclidean geometry when I was twelve. I made such rapid progress that I discovered an original proof of the Pythagorean theorem before My thirteenth birthday.

Books

1.Relativity: The Special and General Theory (1916):

This book provides an accessible explanation of Einstein's theories of special and general relativity. It is written for the layperson and is one of his most famous works.

2.The Meaning of Relativity (1922):

This book expands upon the concepts introduced in his earlier work, delving deeper into the implications of relativity theory for our understanding of space, time, and gravity.

3.The World as I See It (1934):

In this collection of essays and articles, Einstein reflects on a wide range of topics, including science, religion, politics, and human nature. It offers insight into his philosophical and moral perspectives.
